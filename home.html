Frequently Asked Questions

What is SGI Asia? +
SGI Asia (PT. SysTech Global Informasi) is a cybersecurity and IT consulting company based in Jakarta, Indonesia, specializing in delivering end-to-end security services, professional training, and technical implementation support for organizations that require strong protection and security readiness.
What does it mean to be a “CompTIA Authorized Partner”? +
CompTIA is a globally recognized provider of professional IT certifications. Being a CompTIA Authorized Partner means SGI Asia is officially recognized to deliver training and services aligned with international industry standards.
How does SGI ensure data security and client confidentiality? +
We follow strict security protocols, apply encryption, and operate under confidentiality agreements to ensure full protection of client data.
What cybersecurity services does SGI Asia provide? +
SGI Asia provides a wide range of cybersecurity services, including penetration testing or ethical hacking, vulnerability assessment, secure code review, secure software development consulting, digital forensics, data recovery, cybersecurity implementation assistance, professional training programs, virtual labs for hands-on security skills, and managed security monitoring services delivered through trusted partners.
What sets SGI Asia apart from other cybersecurity companies? +
SGI Asia stands out through its comprehensive range of cybersecurity services, strong technical expertise, and the use of industry-standard frameworks. As an authorized CompTIA partner, SGI Asia also provides recognized training supported by practical virtual labs. With experience serving enterprise-level clients, SGI Asia delivers reliable, scalable, and hands-on cybersecurity solutions.
