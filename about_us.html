About Us

About PT. SGI ASIA

At PT. Sytech Global Informasi, we are dedicated to delivering innovative cybersecurity and IT solutions tailored to meet the unique needs of modern businesses. Our expertise covers a broad range of services, including penetration testing, digital forensics, secure code review, server migration, data recovery, and IT training. We proactively strengthen cybersecurity with robust defenses, advanced threat detection, and comprehensive compliance strategies to help minimize risks and enhance organizational resilience.

Our customized solutions are designed to protect, optimize, and transform IT infrastructures, ensuring maximum reliability and performance. Security is our top priority — we empower businesses with the protection and agility they need to thrive in today’s rapidly evolving digital landscape.
